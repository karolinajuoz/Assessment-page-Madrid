Madrid, ✈︎ Spain

About Madrid:

Madrid is the capital of Spain. It was created around the year of 860 A.C, and is one of the greenest cities in Europe. It is a city that offers amazing architecture, world-class galleries, plenty of history, as well as amazing food and nightlife.

My experience:

I spent two unforgettable weeks in Madrid in April 2022. If you prefer a quieter experience with fewer tourists, November or March are ideal months to visit, offering lower costs and a more intimate feel of the city. However, if you do not mind this, and you thrive in the energy of a bustling city, visiting during spring or late summer also provides a vibrant atmosphere. My time in Madrid was incredible, I would definitely say it is an essential stop when visiting Europe!
